Negative Impacts

1.Facility closures, social isolation, and quarantine have caused a loss of social connection with teachers, friends, and peers.

2. Decreased physical activity, loss of tutor time, and increased 'screen time' through virtual learning, social media can affect mental health.

3.Covid has caused isolation, loneliness and illness which affect the physical health.

4.The pandemic has resulted in job loss and financial instability which has caused a lot of people to grief.

5.The virus has affected around 1.6 billion students as they cannot go to school and study.

6.Covid also decreases business opportunities and phyical companies are in loss.

7.During this period, things have become rather pricey and inflation is a major problem.

8.The pandemic causes depression and anxiety which is very harmful to younger generations.
